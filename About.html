Dot & Key

Dot & Key is a skincare brand known for creating gentle, effective, and ingredient-focused products. Their formulations often highlight clean and skin-friendly components, designed to address concerns such as hydration, pigmentation, acne care, and sun protection.

Popular Product Categories

  • Serums (Vitamin C, Niacinamide, Hyaluronic Acid)
  • Moisurizers & Gel Creams
  • Sunscreens
  • Body Care Products
  • Lip Care

Brand Highlights

Dot & Key focuses on lightweight textures and dermatologically tested ingredients. Their products are known for being non-greasy, effective, and suitable for various skin types, especially in humid climates.
