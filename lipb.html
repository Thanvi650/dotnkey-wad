Dot & Key Lip Balm

Product Description

Dot & Key Lip Balm is a nourishing and hydrating lip care formula designed to keep your lips soft, smooth, and supple throughout the day. Enriched with natural butters and essential oils, this lip balm helps restore moisture while providing a gentle shine.

Key Features

  • Deep hydration with shea butter and cocoa butter
  • Infused with natural oils for long-lasting softness
  • Lightweight, non-sticky texture
  • Available in multiple flavors and tints
  • Dermatologically tested and gentle for daily use

Benefits

Regular use helps repair dry, chapped lips and protects them from environmental damage. The blend of nourishing ingredients ensures smoother, healthier-looking lips over time.

How to Use

Apply a thin layer evenly across your lips. Reapply whenever needed for continuous hydration.

Ingredients

Shea Butter, Cocoa Butter, Coconut Oil, Almond Oil, Vitamin E, Plant-Based Wax, Natural Flavors.
